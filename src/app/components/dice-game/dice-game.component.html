DICE GAME

Step 1: Choosing Your Mode
When you first start the game, you'll have three exciting modes to choose from: 1 line, 3 lines, and 5 lines. Each mode offers different ways to win. The more lines you play, the higher your chances of hitting a winning combination, but it also costs more to spin. So, choose wisely!
Step 2: Hitting Play
Once you've picked your preferred mode, it's time to spin the reels. To do this, simply click the "Play" button. This will set the slot machine in motion, and the reels will start spinning.
Step 3: Watch the Reels
As the reels spin, keep a close eye on the symbols. Your goal is to line up the same icons on the active lines, depending on the mode you selected. Here's a quick reminder: In 1 Line mode, you'll need to match symbols on the single middle line to win.
In 3 Line mode, watch for matching symbols on any of the three horizontal lines.
In 5 Line mode, you have even more chances to win with five active lines!
Step 4: Winning Combinations
If the reels stop with the same symbols lined up on one of your active lines, congratulations! You've won! The game will automatically calculate your winnings and add them to your total.
Step 5: Auto Mode (Optional)
If you want to sit back and enjoy the show without clicking "Play" every time, you can use the Auto Mode. Just click the "Auto" button, and the game will spin the reels for you automatically. You can stop this mode at any time by clicking "Stop Auto."
